About

I started my cooking career in Vancouver at a sports restaurant. After realizing that I loved cooking. I attended a year long course at VCC, in which led me to the VTCC.

Having completing collage and a short internship at the Trade and Convention Center, I found myself on the Rocky Mountaineer Train. Which I quite enjoyed traveling back and forth from Vancouver to Calgary or Jasper via Kamloops. Being on the Rocky Mountaineer was a lot of fun, but when an opportunity arose to head back to the Trade and Convention Center for the 2010 winter Olympics I took it.

After the Olympics and having traveled on the Rocky Mountaineer. I decided I wanted to move out to the rockies and to see what living there would be like. I decided on Banff and moved there where I picked up a job working in a pub. But by winter I wanted to be working at one of the ski resorts and ended up at SSV living on the mountain. Near the end of the season I wasn't sure what I was going to do and saw a job add for a cook position at MLL and I took it.

I spent 3 years bouncing from the ski hill in the winter to Moraine Lake in the summer. Living the life, experiencing the joy of freezing glacier fed h2o lakes in the summer and literally frozen h20 in the winter.
